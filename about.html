MAIN CHARACTERS:
Bob Belcher, passed down the family restaurant from his own dad, quite an awkward guy, but a great dad and husband.
Linda Belcher, the quirky, outspoken mom wth many talents and hobbies that she loves sharing with whoever's there to listen.
Tina Belcher, the shy older sister with many crushes and fantasies who also tries her best to not engage with her siblings antics but frequently fails.
Gene Belcher, the only son who has no filter and loves his mom very much who and also has a deep love for music and food.
Louise Belcher, the youngest and sneakiest sibling who tend to get in a lot of trouble and drags her siblings into it, but she means the best most of the time.
